# Apollo Travel Agency Website

![Apollo Travel Agency Preview](./public/portfolio-preview.png)

A modern, responsive website developed for **Apollo Travel Agency**, designed to showcase travel services, build customer trust, and provide a smooth digital experience for travelers, corporate organizations, NGOs, and business clients.

The platform presents Apollo's travel solutions including airline ticketing, hotel reservations, car rental services, and corporate travel management.

---

## 🌍 About Apollo Travel Agency

Established in 2019, Apollo Travel Agency provides reliable and personalized travel solutions connecting Ethiopia with destinations around the world.

The website aims to:

- Strengthen Apollo's online presence
- Showcase travel services
- Improve customer engagement
- Build credibility through testimonials and company information
- Provide an accessible and responsive user experience
